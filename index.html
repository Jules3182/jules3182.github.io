Who Am I?


How are you? My name is Julian Baker. I'm a father who's obsessed with graphic design, home labbing, nerdy tattoos, and hacking together whatever sketchy tech I can get my hands on. I am based in Fort Myers Florida, raising my daughter and working while I explore my many hobbies and try my best to break as few of them as possible. If you are looking for graphic design, drone footage, programming work, PC's built, or a whole host of other nerdy nonsense feel free to contact me via the social icons below! I'm your man.



What do I do?


I am a man of endless interests at varying levels of skill. Including: graphic design (Blender, 2d in Photoshop, Lightroom, Ilustrator, etc.) video editing to homelabbing (Proxmox, Docker, etc.) programming (HTML, CSS, JS, TS, Java, C#, Rust, etc.) robotics (Building FPV Drones, 3D Printing, PC Building).

My goal Ulitmate is to perfectly balance effeciency and aesthetics in all of my projects.
